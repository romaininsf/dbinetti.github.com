About

I'm a six-time entrepreneur, speaker, and consultant on Lean Startup strategy and tactics.

My last company, Votizen, leads a chapter in Eric Ries's #1 Bestseller "The Lean Startup." I included Eric, Steve Blank, Sean Parker, Ashton Kutcher, Peter Thiel, and Ron Conway among my investors and brought Votizen to a successful acquisition in 2013.

I've also been on the founding team of five other startups, including QFN.com, which became Quicken.com (Intuit), Arch Rock (acquired by Cisco), and USA.gov (named USA Today's Site of the Year in 2000.)

I received my Bachelors with Highest Honors and General Distinction from UC Berkeley, and my MBA at UCLA where my team received the Deloitte Consulting Award for Top Thesis in the graduating class. I live in San Carlos, CA, with my wife and two daughters.
